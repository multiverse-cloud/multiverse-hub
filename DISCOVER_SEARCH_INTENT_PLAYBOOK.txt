MULTIVERSE DISCOVER SEARCH INTENT PLAYBOOK
Created: 2026-03-27

This project should not be built only around categories like:
- Movies
- Apps
- AI
- Sports

It must also be built around real search intent patterns.

Core high-traffic formats:
- How to ...
- What is ...
- Best ...
- Top 10 ...
- How to make money ...
- How to start ...
- Free ...
- Near me ...
- ... vs ...
- Tips for ...

Important:
- category is only the container
- search intent format is what makes pages rankable

Examples:

- How to make money online
- How to earn money from home
- Best online jobs for students
- How to start freelancing
- How to make money with AI
